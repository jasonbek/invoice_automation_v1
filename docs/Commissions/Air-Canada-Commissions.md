**Appendix 4 - Terms and Conditions applicable for All Services**

**Applicable Carriers**

-   **Air Canada Carriers:** Air Canada and any carrier with whom Air Canada has a capacity purchase or wet lease agreement operating under the "Air Canada", "Air Canada Rouge" or "Air Canada Express" brands, plus The Landline Company Inc. operating ground transportation for Air Canada.
    
-   **Transatlantic Joint Venture Carriers:** Air Canada Carriers, Deutsche Lufthansa AG, Austrian Airlines Inc., Swiss International Airlines Limited, Brussels Airlines, Edelweiss Air, Discover Airlines, United Airlines Inc., and any wet lease agreement carrier operating on their behalf.
    
-   **China Joint Venture Carriers:** Air Canada Carriers and Air China LTD.
    

**Time of Ticketing APPLIES to:**

-   Original tickets and the additional collection of ticket exchanges.
    
-   Base fare, surcharge, stopover charges and elements shown in the fare box and fare calculation box.
    
-   Published fares sold as one-way, round-trip, open jaw, side trip and end-on-end per fare rules.
    
-   Groups booked in Air Canada's group booking tool (G-Cubed) using published fares for travel within Canada and between Canada and the US.
    
-   Meetings and Conventions tickets.
    
-   Flight Passes purchased at [aircanada.com/agents](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://aircanada.com/agents), including those with a PN, SMB, Air Canada or joint venture corporate contract number in the client identifier box.
    
-   For Transatlantic only (plating on UA): fares using ticket designators starting with 8Dxx for Propel levels 1-4 and Blueprint Discover, Experience or Savings programs, as well as ticket designators FLX3, FLEX, PFLX, FL01, AAxx, AExx or QYxx.
    

**Time of Ticketing DOES NOT apply to:**

-   Change fees, seat selection fees, meals, upgrades, and other optional product/service fees.
    
-   All net fares, including Leisure/Group net fares and (no fare ticketing).
    
-   Small and Medium Business tickets identified with a PN # or any other account number.
    
-   Corporate contract tickets identified with an Air Canada, joint venture corporate contract, or any other corporate contract number in the tour code box (including those with no corporate discount applied).
    
-   Aeroplan redemption tickets, industry reduced rates, Air Canada Vacations air-only tickets, bereavement travel and other discount program fares.
    
-   Taxes, surcharge and any fees/charges entered in the tax boxes.
    
-   Groups booked in G-Cubed using published fares for travel to the Caribbean, Mexico, Central/South America, Transatlantic, mainland China and Transpacific.
    
-   Round the World (RTW) fares.
    
-   Infant fares not occupying a seat.
    
-   Tickets issued via the Air Canada web link.
    
-   Retroactive Time of Ticketing; travel agents must claim the correct time of ticketing upon ticket issuance.
